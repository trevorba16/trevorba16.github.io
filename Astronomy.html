Astronomy

  • Equipment: Telescope, Binoculars, Star charts
  • Weather: Cold and clear

The Experience

Drive as far away from the city as possible, and set up somewhere with a clear view of the night sky.

This requires some advance planning, as the weather will greatly affect the experience, and you should also have a plan for what you hope to see before you get there. Do the research and learn when to go, how to set up, and where to look.
