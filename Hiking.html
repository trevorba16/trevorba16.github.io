Hiking

  • Equipment: Good shoes.
  • Weather: As long as it isn't raining, you'll be fine.

The Experience

Your experience hiking all depends on the trail. For inexperienced hikers, there are shorter trails, with easy terrain. For those who are more experienced, some trails require much more.

When planning a trip hiking, you want to make sure that you pick a time that isn't too hot, or a trail that has shade. You need a length that you can achieve comfortably, and you want a great view or experience once you get to the end.
